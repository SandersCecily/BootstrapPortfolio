About Me

Hello! If you've come to read about me, then lucky for you, you're in the right place. My name is Cecily Sanders and I am a student interested in development. I word it in this ever specific manner because if I were to say web development I would be limiting myself as I also take interest in software development and frankly if I have the opportunity to know how something is made then I most definitely want to know. Currently I am attending the UPenn Coding Bootcamp (shoutout to you guys, hey!) but, I am also a student at Temple University and have my Associates from Community College of Philadelphia. I major undergrad in Computer Science and my Associates was actually in Information Technology and Sciences. I am a bit notorious for wanting to be a jack of all trades and trying to be as useful as possible by being knowledgable about a wide variety of subjects.

Personally I love working with people and computers! I thrive in a team collaborative enviroment but also appreciate the ability to be able to brainstorm and bring my own personality to my work. Some of those reasons are why I now work as a casino dealer but, also drive me away from the floor of the casino to the home of an office space with (hopefully) a set group of diverse individuals getting ready to work hard on any particular challenges that might arise.
